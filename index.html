Privacy Policy Breadcrumbs · Last updated 18 June 2026 Breadcrumbs is built to be private by design. Your information never leaves your phone. What we collect Nothing. Breadcrumbs has no account system, no login and no sign-up. We do not collect, transmit or store any personal information on any server. Where your data lives Everything you enter stays in a local database on your own device. The app works fully offline and does not request internet access. We cannot see your entries, and neither can anyone else. No tracking Breadcrumbs contains no analytics, no advertising and no third-party trackers. Nothing about how you use the app is reported back to us or to anyone else. Your data is yours You can export your entries to CSV or JSON at any time. Uninstalling the app removes your data from your device. Because we never receive it, there is nothing for us to delete on our end. Notifications If you enable the daily reminder, it is scheduled locally on your device. No reminder data is sent anywhere.
