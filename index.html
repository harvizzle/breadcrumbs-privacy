Privacy Policy

Breadcrumbs · Last updated June 26, 2026

Overview

Breadcrumbs is private by design. It is a local-first Android app for daily lifestyle tracking and personal statistics.

Breadcrumbs has no account, no login or sign-up, no ads, no analytics, no tracking, no backend, no cloud sync, and no Internet permission.

What Breadcrumbs stores locally

Breadcrumbs stores app data in a local database on your device. This can include:

  • daily entries and metric values
  • custom metrics, labels, colors, and ordering
  • milestones and optional notes
  • settings, reminder preferences, and language preference

This data stays on your device unless you choose to export it.

What we collect

We do not receive your Breadcrumbs data. The app does not send your entries, metrics, milestones, notes, settings, reminder preferences, or language preference to us or to a Breadcrumbs server.

Since we never receive your user data, there is no server-side user data for us to access or delete.

No tracking or ads

Breadcrumbs does not include advertising, analytics, tracking, crash-reporting, or cloud-sync services. The app does not request Android Internet permission.

Exports and backups

Exporting is optional and user-initiated. JSON export/import can include entries, metric definitions, milestones, and notes. CSV export is for metric values.

Exported files only leave Breadcrumbs when you choose a destination through Android’s share sheet. The app or service you choose to receive an export may have its own privacy practices.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, they are scheduled locally on your device. Reminder settings are stored locally and are not sent to us.

External links

Breadcrumbs may link to the developer’s public creator/profile page, such as Linktree. External websites are outside Breadcrumbs and may have their own privacy practices.

Deleting your data

You can edit or delete data inside the app. Clearing app data or uninstalling Breadcrumbs removes local app data from your device. Copies you exported elsewhere are controlled by the destination you chose.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new last updated date.
